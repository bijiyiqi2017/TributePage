Ralph C. Smedley

The Visionary Leader Behind Toastmasters

A Legacy of Empowering Communicators

Ralph C. Smedley, born in 1878 in Waverly, Illinois, had a profound impact on the world of public speaking and leadership.

  • •
    1905: Establishes the first Toastmasters club in Santa Ana, California, sparking a global movement.
  • •
    1924: Proposes the idea of Toastmasters International, a platform for personal and professional development.
  • •
    1960: The Toastmasters International headquarters moves to Rancho Santa Margarita, California, becoming a hub for communication excellence.
  • •
    1965: Ralph C. Smedley leaves a lasting legacy, passing away, but his vision continues to inspire millions worldwide.

"We learn best in moments of enjoyment."

-- Ralph C. Smedley
